Export & Logistics

Ports We Use

  • Chennai — frequent destination for southern India
  • Nhava Sheva (JNPT, Mumbai) — large container hub for western India
  • Mundra — efficient for parts of western India
  • Goa — for regional buyers and smaller shipments

Documentation

We prepare: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading (B/L), Certificate of Origin, Inspection Reports (if required), and weight/ticket records. Incoterms available as per agreement (FOB / CIF / CFR etc.).

Logistics Services

  • Container stuffing / stuffing supervision
  • Third-party pre-shipment inspection (on request)
  • Local agent coordination for customs clearance
  • Scheduling and consolidation of smaller loads

Typical Lead Times

Lead times vary by order size and shipping schedule. We provide shipment ETAs once allocation and vessel booking is confirmed.
