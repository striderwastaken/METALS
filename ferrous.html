Ferrous Scrap — HMS 1 & HMS 2

Products

We supply HMS 1 & HMS 2 classified ferrous scrap suitable for re-rolling & melting. Our material is sourced and graded in the UK and loaded under supervision.

  • HMS 1: Heavier, cleaner scrap usually from structural sources.
  • HMS 2: Mixed scrap including shredded or lighter scrap.

Quality & Loading

Each consignment is inspected. Buyers may request pre-shipment photos, weighbridge tickets and third-party inspection certificates. We offer supervised loading to minimise claims on arrival.

Typical Volume & Packaging

Shipments range from LCL to full containers (FCL). For bulk shipments we arrange container stuffing or direct vessel loads where applicable, with standard commercial invoices, packing lists and bills of lading provided.

Ports of Discharge

We coordinate discharges primarily at Chennai, Nhava Sheva (JNPT), Mundra and Goa with experienced local agents.
