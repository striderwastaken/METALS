Who we are

SchemaShine Metals is a UK-based exporter and trader of ferrous & non-ferrous scrap. Established 15+ years ago, we specialise in HMS 1 & 2, aluminium scrap, brass, copper and other recyclable materials — shipped primarily to Chennai, Nhava Sheva (Mumbai), Goa, Mundra and major global ports.
